Merge LinkedList--->You are given two LinkedList of length ‘N’. Your task is to insert the elements of the second LinkedList in the first LinkedList at the alternate positions. For example: Let 1 -> 3 -> 5 be the first LinkedList and 2 -> 4 -> 6 be the second LinkedList. Then after merging the first LinkedList will look like 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4 -> 5 -> 6.
